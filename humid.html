Humidity


This is a plot comparing city latitude to it's recorded humidity on a given day. There does not appear to be much correlation between humidity and latitude. Humidity is likely influenced by other geographical features such as proximity to water bodies, mountain ranges, vegetation, elevation and winds to name a few.
