Max Temperature


This is a plot comparing city latitude to it's maximum recorded temperature on a given day. As we can see, there appears to be a correlation between latitude and maximum temperature. Cities located closer to the equator tend to have higher recoreded temperatures. This data was also recored in January, which would be the wwinter months for the southern hemisphere. For this reason we tend to see warmer weather in the southern latitudes compared to the northern latitudes.
